SIZE GUIDE

Measurements of Friday Originals menswear sets. Choose your standard size.

SIZE CHEST (IN) LENGTH (IN) SHOULDER (IN) SLEEVE (IN)
M 40" 28" 18" 8.5"
L 42" 29" 19" 9"
XL 44" 30" 20" 9.5"
HOW TO MEASURE
  • Chest: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal.
  • Length: Measure from the highest point of the shoulder down to the hem.
  • Shoulder: Measure straight across the back from shoulder point to shoulder point.
